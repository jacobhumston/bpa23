Become Part of our Crew!

Want to become part of the Game Day Grill crew? We are always looking for new people to join our team. If you wish to apply, contact us using our phone number or email and we will get to you as soon as possible within work hours. When you call or email, specify that you are applying for a job and we will get you started on the application process. Our contact information can be found on our 'Contact Us' page.

The current positions we are hiring for are: Game Day Grill Crew Member and Game Day Grill Manager.


Overall Job Requirements

  • You must be 16 years or older
  • You must be a legal US Citizen shown with a valid ID

Manager Job Requirements

  • You must be 18 years or older
  • You must have allotted at least 520 hours of working at any Game Day Grill location in the last two years

While working at Game Day Grill, you may have to learn an assortment of skills. Typical skills you may learn are: preparing food, serving food to patrons, cleaning the restaurant, working as a cashier, and answering any questions, emails, and phone calls you may receive.

Remember to provide your name, address, contact information, and position of interest when you contact us.
