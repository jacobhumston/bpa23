Party Room Reservations

Want to reserve a party room? Party rooms are only to be reserved for times within store hours. Furthermore, if another party room is booked on the day you want to book yours, the party you book must start at least two hours after the previous one ended, or two hours before the other one starts so our staff can recooperate and provide you the best possible service. To reserve a party room, a two week notice is needed. Rates run at $11.99/per kid under 12, and $14.99/per adult. You can reserve a party room by contacting us through our email or phone number.
